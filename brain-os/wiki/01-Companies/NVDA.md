**Sector:** Technology
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-10

## Summary

NVIDIA designs and sells GPUs and system-on-chip units. Dominant AI accelerator vendor through its CUDA ecosystem and H/B-series data center chips.

## Investment Thesis

NVIDIA is the infrastructure layer for the AI buildout. CUDA moat, data center pricing power, and software ecosystem lock-in create durable competitive advantage as AI CapEx scales.

## Bull Case

- AI CapEx cycle is multi-year; hyperscalers remain supply-constrained
- CUDA ecosystem creates deep switching costs
- Expanding into networking (InfiniBand/NVLink), software (NIM), and robotics (Isaac)

## Bear Case

- Custom silicon (Google TPU, AWS Trainium, Meta MTIA) could reduce dependency
- Export restrictions limit China TAM
- Valuation already prices in perfection
