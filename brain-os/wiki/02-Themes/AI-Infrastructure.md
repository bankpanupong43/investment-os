# AI Infrastructure

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-10
**Source:** manual

---

## Summary

The buildout of compute, networking, and software infrastructure required to train and serve large AI models. Multi-year CapEx cycle driven by hyperscalers and sovereigns.

## Key Companies

- [[NVDA]] — GPU compute monopoly, CUDA ecosystem
- [[TSM]] — foundry for all leading AI chips
- [[MSFT]] — Azure AI, OpenAI partnership
- [[GOOG]] — TPU development, Gemini, GCP
- [[AMZN]] — AWS Trainium/Inferentia, Bedrock

## Opportunities

- Continued hyperscaler AI CapEx through 2027
- Sovereign AI programs (Middle East, Europe, Japan)
- Edge inference as models shrink

## Risks

- Custom silicon reducing NVDA TAM
- AI ROI disappointment triggers CapEx pullback
- Export restrictions limiting revenue
